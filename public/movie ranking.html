Top Films Ever by Pols (2023 version)

My Top 3 Films Watched in 2023

Thirst (2009)

Sang-hyeon is a priest. His life changes for the worse when he willingly participates in a medical experiment and gets affected by vampirism.

Poor Things (2023)

Brought back to life by an unorthodox scientist, a young woman runs off with a lawyer on a whirlwind adventure across the continents. Free from the prejudices of her times, she grows steadfast in her purpose to stand for equality and liberation.

Nope (2023)

A man and his sister discover something sinister in the skies above their California horse ranch, while the owner of a nearby theme park tries to profit from the mysterious, otherworldly phenomenon.
